# Short Pitch

I created a modern black-and-white website concept for **Fit 24 Gyms**, a local fitness business. The purpose of the website is to help the gym look more professional online, build trust with potential customers, and make it easier for people to learn about services, schedules, pricing, and contact details.

This website helps the business by putting all the important information in one place. Instead of customers needing to message the gym for basic details, they can quickly see memberships, training services, class times, and how to get in touch.

The site is also mobile-friendly, which is important because many customers search for local businesses on their phones. By adding clear call-to-action buttons such as **Book a Free Trial**, **Call**, and **WhatsApp**, the website increases the chance of turning visitors into paying members.

This project is designed like a real client proposal, showing how a simple website can help a local gym grow its online presence and attract more customers.
